SWAuto is an open source Summoner's War coupons auto redeemer. It is by no mean an official app of Com2Us WithHive. It scrapes the coupon data from [swq.jp](https://swq.jp/l/en-US/) and redeem it on the [official com2us coupon redeem website](https://event.withhive.com/ci/smon/evt_coupon). It uses Flask for backend and Render's PostgreSQL service to store the data.
